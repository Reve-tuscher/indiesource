Low-MOQ skincare manufacturers

Finding a manufacturer that will take a small order is the hardest part of launching a beauty brand. Most factories want 1,000+ units per product. These do not.

Below is a shortlist of contract and private-label manufacturers that publicly work with indie skincare and cosmetics brands at low or no minimums. Use it as a starting point for your own outreach — then confirm the current MOQ, per-unit price and certifications directly before you commit.

How to choose between them

Shortlist three to five, request samples from each, and compare on four things: the real minimum per SKU, the per-unit cost at that minimum, who owns safety and compliance for your market, and reorder lead times. The cheapest minimum is not always the cheapest launch.

What is a "low" MOQ for skincare?

Anything from no minimum up to about 100–500 units per product. Several labs above start at 10–50 units or have no minimum at all.

Are low-MOQ manufacturers more expensive per unit?

Usually yes — smaller runs cost more each. The trade-off is far less upfront cash and risk while you validate demand.

Can I get custom formulas at low MOQ?

Some offer stock formulas you label (lowest minimums) and custom formulation (higher minimums). Start with stock to launch faster.
